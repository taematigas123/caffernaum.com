Relationship Summary

From Cardinality To Link Notes
Firebase Auth 1 to 1 members auth.uid = members.uid One account maps to one profile document.
members 1 to N orders orders.memberUid Member orders are linked; guest orders may use null.
orders.items N to M inventory items[].product ~ inventory.name Conceptual ingredient dependency, not a strict foreign key.
staff N to N orders, inventory Operational Staff users manage statuses and stock workflows.

Customer ER Diagram (index.html)

localStorage.customerState

  • PK: memberUid (optional)
  • memberLoggedIn
  • caffeCart[]
  • rewardPoints

Firestore.orders

  • PK: id
  • memberUid (optional FK)
  • items[] + total
  • customer + addressDetails
Customer cart and rewards are local first, then checkout writes normalized order records to Firestore.
